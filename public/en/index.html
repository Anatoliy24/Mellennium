20.06.2017
According to the tenor of the related draft law posted on the official portal of the draft regulations, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation proposes to introduce an administrative fine to be imposed on the companies and executive officers which have not taken care of timely departure of the foreigners invited by them, from the country.

In an open hearing, the Constitutional Court of the Russian Federation ruled the provisions of the Russian migration-related law, which oblige the foreigners to be registered at a place of stay, as unconstitutional and inconsistent with the Constitution of Russia.
15.07.2017
The paper migration cards under the seal of the Border Guard, which are issued to the foreigners when entering the Russian Federation, will be superseded by their electronic versions, i.e., by the digital migration cards in the foreseeable future.
