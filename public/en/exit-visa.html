EXIT VISAS

For the visiting of a variety of countries of the world, the citizens of the Russian Federation, as well as the foreigners employed in Russia, need the related visas. Visa is a permit for stay in the country within a given time.
  • A Visa contains such particulars as: the time period within which an entry into the country is possible, and within which the last exit from the country must have been carried out; the number of entries into the country; and the permitted duration of stay in the country.
  • A Visa is processed before the beginning of travel at a Consulate of the concerned country within the territory of Russia, and is to be glued in the Passport.
